This website represents "בית מדרש ספרא" (Beit Midrash Safra), a Jewish learning and community center located in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Beit Midrash Safra is primarily dedicated to Torah study, Jewish learning, and community-building for Israeli soldiers, national service members, and young adults during their period of service.

########################################
# ADDITIONAL PROGRAM (SUB-ENTITY)
########################################

As part of its activity, Beit Midrash Safra also hosts an additional learning track:

"משב רוח" (Mashav Ruach)

Mashav Ruach is a parallel weekly learning program held on Mondays, operating under the framework of Beit Midrash Safra.

The program is based on joint learning led by a mixed secular and religious educational team and integrates diverse sources from the Jewish bookshelf — from Talmudic texts to contemporary philosophy.

Mashav Ruach is an additional program within Beit Midrash Safra and should not be treated as a separate or independent institution.
