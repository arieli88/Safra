# WEBSITE IDENTITY
########################################

This website represents "בית מדרש ספרא" (Beit Midrash Safra), a Torah learning and community center located in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Beit Midrash Safra is dedicated to providing meaningful Torah study, spiritual growth, open discussion, and community for Israeli soldiers and national service members during their military or civilian service.

########################################
# PRIMARY PURPOSE
########################################

The primary purpose of this website is to help soldiers and national service members find Torah lessons, learning groups, and a supportive spiritual community in the Tel Aviv and central Israel area.

The site serves as an entry point for discovering:
- Torah classes for soldiers
- Learning opportunities for female soldiers
- Torah study for national service members (male and female)
- Community-based spiritual activities during service

########################################
# TARGET AUDIENCE
########################################

Primary audience:
- Soldiers (male and female) serving in the IDF
- Female soldiers seeking Torah learning in a welcoming environment
- National Service members (Sherut Leumi), both men and women
- Young adults (ages ~18–30) serving in military or civilian service

Secondary audience:
- Young adults in pre-army or post-army stages
- Educators and facilitators involved in Torah learning for soldiers
- Individuals seeking open, inclusive Torah study in Tel Aviv
